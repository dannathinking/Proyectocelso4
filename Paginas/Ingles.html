Hello everyone! Today, I’ll explain the structure of our webpage. This site is designed to present educational content in an organized and interactive way. At the top, we have a fixed banner with a gradient background and a school logo. This keeps the identity of the website visible at all times.
(Danna)
Below, there's a button panel that serves as the main navigation. Each button links to a different subject page, like Physics, Chemistry, English, and more. Moving down, we have the content section. This includes an image box displaying the school logo and a text section meant for an introduction or a welcome message.
(Daniel)
The layout is responsive, meaning that on small screens, content stacks in a column format, while on larger screens, elements arrange in rows for a better experience. Further down, we find a carousel that showcases multiple images, adding a dynamic touch to the site. Users can navigate through the images using arrows.
(Celso)
Lastly, to enhance the design, we’ve included floating bubbles that animate in the background, creating a modern and engaging visual effect. That’s a quick overview of our webpage’s structure! It’s clean, interactive, and mobile-friendly. Thank you for watching!
